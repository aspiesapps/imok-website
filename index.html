App Functionaliteit in Beeld

Contact Information (READ_CONTACTS permission)

The Application requests permission to read your contacts. This permission is used only when you, the user, actively choose to select a contact from your address book to set up an emergency action (SMS or Email). The selected phone number or email address is then stored securely and locally on your device's private storage.

This information is never transmitted, shared, or uploaded to any server. It is only used by the Application on your device to send the configured emergency messages.

SMS Permission (SEND_SMS)

The Application uses the SEND_SMS permission to autonomously send an emergency alert to your pre-defined contacts if a daily check-in is missed. This is the core safety feature of the app and functions entirely locally.

In-App Donations

The Application offers the ability to make voluntary donations through the Google Play Store's billing system. All financial transactions are handled securely by Google. We do not receive, process, or store any of your personal payment information.

Anonymous Usage Data

We do not collect any personal or anonymous usage data. All app functionality and user data remain on your device.

Battery Optimization

To ensure the reliability of the safety check, the app may request to ignore battery optimizations. This allows the AlarmManager to trigger notifications even when the device is in standby mode.

If you have any questions regarding privacy while using the Application, please contact us via email at aspies.apps@gmail.com.
