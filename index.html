An autonomous safety net for you and your pets.

The Story Behind the App

I'm OK was developed out of personal necessity: caring for a loyal companion. For those who live alone, the biggest concern isn't what happens to yourself, but what happens to those who depend on you if you are suddenly gone.

This app ensures that help is summoned immediately when you can no longer respond, so your pet is never left unnoticed and alone.

Privacy & Permissions

This app is built for maximum reliability and privacy. No data is collected or shared. Everything stays on your phone.

SEND_SMS

This permission is the core of the emergency plan. If you miss a check-in, the app automatically sends SMS messages to your pre-selected contacts directly from your device. We chose SMS because it is independent of an internet connection, ensuring a reliable alert even in areas with poor data coverage. Since the app sends messages locally and not through a central server, your data remains 100% private.

READ_CONTACTS

To make setting up your emergency plan easy and error-free, the app requests permission to read your contact list. This allows you to select emergency contacts directly from your address book, avoiding typos. We do not scan your contacts in the background or store your address book; only the contacts you manually select are saved locally on your device.

Battery Optimization

Modern smartphones have aggressive power-saving features that can "silence" apps to save battery. For a safety app like I'm OK, this is dangerous. By ignoring battery optimization, the app remains "awake" in the background—just like a standard alarm clock—ensuring that check-in timers and reminders are delivered with 100% reliability exactly on time.

Full-Screen Notifications

To ensure that you never miss a critical check-in alert, the app requests permission to display high-priority notifications that can temporarily take over the screen. This works just like an alarm clock or an incoming phone call, making sure the alert is seen immediately. This is essential for the core safety function of the app.
